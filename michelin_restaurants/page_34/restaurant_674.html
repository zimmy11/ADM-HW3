Al Capitan della Cittadella

piazza Cittadella 7/a, Verona, 37122, Italy
€€€ · Seafood, Italian Contemporary
Situated just outside the town walls, Al Capitan has been one of the most renowned fish restaurants in Verona for over fifteen years. Classic Italian dishes with a contemporary twist added by the owner-chef take pride of place here, with an emphasis on top-quality ingredients. The wine list includes a good selection of French wines, with a particular focus on champagnes.
